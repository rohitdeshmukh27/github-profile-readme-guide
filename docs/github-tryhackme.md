# 🔐 TryHackMe Badge — Cybersecurity Badge for GitHub

> Display your TryHackMe rank, points, and progress on your GitHub profile with an auto-updating badge.

---

## 📖 Overview

[TryHackMe](https://tryhackme.com) is a popular cybersecurity learning platform. Their badge system lets you display your rank and progress directly on your GitHub profile — perfect for security researchers, ethical hackers, and CTF players.

---

## ✨ What It Does

- Shows your **current TryHackMe rank**
- Displays your **points earned**
- Shows **rooms completed**
- Auto-updates with your progress
- No setup required beyond having a TryHackMe account

---

## 🚀 Installation & Setup

Add this to your README:

```html
<img src="https://tryhackme-badges.s3.amazonaws.com/torvalds.png" alt="TryHackMe Badge"/>
```

> 📝 **Note:** Replace `torvalds` with your TryHackMe username.

## ✅ Pros
- Zero setup — just your username
- Auto-updates with your progress
- Professional look for security profiles
- Shows real achievements

## ❌ Cons
- Only available for TryHackMe users
- Badge design is not customizable
- Image hosted on AWS (potential loading delays)
- Limited to rank and points display

## 🎯 Best Use Cases
- Cybersecurity professionals
- CTF (Capture The Flag) players
- Security researchers
- Students learning ethical hacking
