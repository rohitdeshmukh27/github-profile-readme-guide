## 📝 How to Submit Your Profile

We'd love to feature your GitHub profile in our showcase! Here's how:

### Requirements

- ✅ Your profile must be **public**
- ✅ Your profile must use at least **2 tools** from this guide
- ✅ Your profile should look **polished and professional**
- ❌ No NSFW content
- ❌ No spam or self-promotion beyond the showcase entry

### Steps

1. **Fork** this repository
2. **Edit** this file (`SHOWCASE.md`)
3. **Add your entry** under the appropriate category in this format:

```markdown
| [@yourusername](https://github.com/yourusername) | Brief description of your profile style and tools used |
```

4. **Submit a Pull Request** with the title: `Showcase: Add @yourusername`

### Example Submission

```markdown
| [@johndoe](https://github.com/johndoe) | Minimalist dark theme with Skill Icons, Streak Stats, and Activity Graph |
```
